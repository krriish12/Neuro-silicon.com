# Formspree Setup Guide - Simple Form Backend

## What is Formspree?
Formspree handles form submissions for you. All data is stored in their dashboard, and you can export to Excel/CSV anytime.

## Step 1: Create Formspree Account

1. Go to https://formspree.io/
2. Click **Get Started** (top right)
3. Sign up with your email: **careers@neuro-silicon.com**
4. Verify your email

## Step 2: Create New Form

1. After login, click **+ New Project**
2. Name it: **Neuro-Silicon**
3. Click **+ New Form**
4. Name it: **Contact Form**
5. Copy the **Form Endpoint** - it looks like:
   ```
   https://formspree.io/f/xyzabc123
   ```

## How to View Submissions:

1. Login to https://formspree.io/
2. Click on your form
3. See all submissions in a table
4. Click **Export** → **Download CSV** → Open in Excel

## How to Export to Excel:

1. In Formspree dashboard, click your form
2. Click **Export** button (top right)
3. Choose **CSV** format
4. Download the file
5. Open with Excel
